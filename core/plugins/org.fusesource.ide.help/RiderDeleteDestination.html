Deleting a JMS destination

Overview

When testing failover scenarios or other scenarios that involve handling failures, it's convenient to be able to easily remove a JMS destination. The tooling has a menu option that allows you to remove a destination with a single click.

Procedure

To delete a JMS destination:

  1. In JMX Navigator, select the JMS destination you want to delete listed under the Queues or the Topics child.

  2. Right-click it to open the context menu, and then select Delete Queue/Topic.
